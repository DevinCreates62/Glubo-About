Our Story

Founded in 1973 in the heart of San Francisco, GLUBO began as a small toy company with big dreams. What started as a modest venture in children's entertainment would soon revolutionize the gaming industry with groundbreaking innovations like the Globob computer system.

Our journey from a local toy manufacturer to a global gaming powerhouse is marked by countless innovations, beloved game franchises like Blario Sisters, and a commitment to pushing the boundaries of interactive entertainment.
